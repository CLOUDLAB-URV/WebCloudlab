HousingRAG

  • Start date:
  • End date:
  • Funded by: Chair of Housing (URV)
  • Ref:

The HousingRAG project, developed by CloudLab research group and the UNESCO Chair of Housing, both at Rovira i Virgili University, aims to develop a specialized and reliable RAG (Real-Time Analysis Group) on housing, geared towards teaching, research, and communication. Through this, we intend for HousingRAG to be a useful tool for the ex-ante and ex-post analysis of the legal framework for housing, taking into account reality (evidence-based policy), so that it can ultimately support students, researchers, professionals, technicians, policymakers, and citizens in their decision-making.
