date: 2025-12-22

The CloudLab research group at the URV has been recognized as one of the 22 winners of the Aina Challenge thanks to PACAT (Platform for Conversational Agents with Aina Kit), a project aimed at creating personalized conversational agents from documents and web pages using advanced Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) techniques. The platform integrates native support for Catalan and will initially be validated within the university community to support learning and academic consultation.

Read the [URV's news][urv-news] for more information (in Catalan).
