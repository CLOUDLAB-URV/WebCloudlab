date: 2025-11-27

The CLOUDLESS research project has held its final event at the Universitat Rovira i Virgili (URV), bringing together researchers, companies, and academic partners to present the results achieved after two years of work. The project focused on designing new cloud and edge computing architectures aimed at improving data processing efficiency in distributed environments, enabling a continuous computing model where tasks are executed either in data centres or directly on devices depending on performance, cost, and privacy requirements.
