title: URV hosts the 29th Conference on Concurrency and Distributed Systems
date: 2023-06-22
---
The University Rovira i Virgili (URV) hosted the 29th edition of the Conference on Concurrency and Distributed Systems, organized by the CloudLab research group in collaboration with the Spanish Society of Concurrent and Distributed Computing. The event brought together researchers, academics, and industry professionals to present and discuss recent advances in areas such as concurrent programming, real-time systems, modelling, analysis, and control of distributed systems, as well as programming languages and architectures. The conference also served as a networking platform to foster collaboration between universities, research centres, and companies, including participation from major firms such as Telefónica and Dell.
